Cloudflare maintains an internal directory of [verified bots and agents](/bots/concepts/bot/verified-bots/) that are associated with search engine optimization (SEO), website monitoring, user-driven automation, and more.

You can use this directory to prevent any bot protection measures from impacting otherwise helpful bots and agents, such as search crawlers.

For a partial list of verified bots and agents, refer to [Cloudflare Radar](https://radar.cloudflare.com/verified-bots).
